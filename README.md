<h1>🌍 Disaster Management Portal</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
The Disaster Management Portal is a web-based platform that provides information,
updates, and safety tips related to natural disasters. The portal helps users stay
informed and prepared by displaying disaster-related news, prevention tips,
and emergency contact information.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Objectives</h2>
<ul>
<li>Provide real-time disaster-related news updates.</li>
<li>Create awareness about different natural disasters.</li>
<li>Share prevention and safety tips.</li>
<li>Display important emergency contact information.</li>
<li>Allow users to contact the portal through a form.</li>
</ul>

<h2>🛠 Technologies Used</h2>
<ul>
<li>HTML5</li>
<li>CSS3</li>
<li>JavaScript</li>
<li>Google Maps API</li>
<li>News API</li>
</ul>

<h2>⚙️ Features</h2>

<h3>📰 News Feed</h3>
<p>
Fetches disaster-related news using News API and displays the latest
headlines with their sources.
</p>

<h3>🗺 Google Maps Integration</h3>
<p>
An interactive map that displays a location using Google Maps API.
This helps visualize disaster-prone or affected areas.
</p>

<h3>🚨 Disaster Awareness Section</h3>
<p>The portal provides information about various disasters such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Earthquake</li>
<li>Flood</li>
<li>Landslide</li>
<li>Wildfires</li>
</ul>

<h3>🛡 Prevention Tips</h3>
<p>
Provides guidance and safety tips to help users prepare for natural disasters.
</p>

<h3>📩 Contact Form</h3>
<p>
Users can submit their name, email, and message to contact the
portal administrators.
</p>

<h3>☎ Helpline Section</h3>
<p>
Displays emergency helpline numbers for quick disaster assistance.
</p>
